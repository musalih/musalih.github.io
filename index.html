About

RaKalid is derived the artists real name Khalid Shams, two words to mean “Forever Sun.” The name RaKalid can be seen as a testament to the way in which he lives his life. In RaKalid’s world, the sun is forever shining through his optimism and positivity for life. In addition to being extremely passionate and dedicated to music, he always strives to see the best in people. He considers music as a tool to inspire change and influence others.
